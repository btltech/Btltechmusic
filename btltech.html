About BTLTECH Music

Music, tools, and artist worlds

BTLTECH Music is an independent UK-based music project created by BTLTECH, blending Afrobeats, Afro-fusion, dancehall, and street anthem energy. The sound is inspired by Lagos roots, London life, hustle culture, family, faith, celebration, and everyday stories of people trying to build something better.

The BTLTECH music journey connects creativity with technology. Alongside releasing songs, BTLTECH works in tech, repairs devices, builds digital tools, and experiments with AI-powered music creation. That mix of hardware, software, music, and visual creativity gives the brand its direction.

BTLTECH is also the creator of Lyrid, a Mac app built to help artists create high-quality lyric videos, visualizers, and music content. Lyrid came from a real need: independent artists need better tools to make professional visuals without expensive teams or complicated software.

The project also introduces Choko King, a faceless Afro-dancehall persona under the BTLTECH Music world. Choko King represents the rougher, bolder side of the brand: deep ragga-style delivery, Nigerian pidgin attitude, Jamaican-inspired dancehall energy, chocolate-and-gold visuals, and street-ready hooks.
