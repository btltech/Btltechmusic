BTLTECH

Afro-fusion. Dancehall energy. Lagos-to-London stories.

BTLTECH is an Afro-fusion artist creating energetic Afrobeats, dancehall, motivational music, and family celebration anthems. His sound connects Lagos street energy with diaspora life — catchy rhythms, pidgin expression, and uplifting stories of ambition, faith, and celebration.
