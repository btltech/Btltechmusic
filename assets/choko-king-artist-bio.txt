Full Bio

Choko King is the sound of two cities at once — the heat, hustle and noise of Lagos pressed against the grey cold of London. A faceless figure in a gold crown, he toasts in a deep, raw gravel voice that slides between Nigerian pidgin and Jamaican patois, turning street life into anthems. Sweet name, dangerous flavour.

His music is Afro-dancehall at its core — ragga toasting, Afrobeats bounce and dancehall riddims — moving from sweaty club bangers to conscious roots reflections without ever losing the swagger. Whether it is turn-up energy for the dancefloor or a hard truth about the hustle, every track carries the same signature: rugged, melodic, and unmistakably him.

Choko King is a virtual artist, created and produced by BTLTECH Music. Like Gorillaz or Hatsune Miku before him, he is a character brought to life — a persona with a world, a voice and a story, built openly rather than hidden. The crown stays on; the face stays off. The focus is the music and the myth.

He arrives with his debut single "Sweet Fire," out 26 June 2026 — the first chapter of a growing catalogue of Afro-dancehall anthems. Lagos-born in spirit, London-cold in delivery, crown never melting: Choko King is building a world one record at a time.

About BTLTECH Music

BTLTECH Music is an independent music and technology studio. Alongside developing Lyrid, its lyric-video creation tool, BTLTECH creates and produces original artists — including the virtual Afro-dancehall act Choko King, whose releases double as a living showcase of what BTLTECH's tools can do.
