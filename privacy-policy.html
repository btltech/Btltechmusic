Privacy Policy

This page explains how BTLTECH Music collects and uses information for music, press, and newsletter communications.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you provide directly when contacting BTLTECH Music or subscribing to release alerts.

  • Email address for release notifications and updates.
  • Contact details submitted for press, booking, or collaboration.
  • Data provided by you through linked third-party platforms.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to respond to enquiries, share music and release updates, and manage artist communication.

  • Send news about new releases, videos, and artist activity.
  • Reply to press, booking, and collaboration requests.
  • Provide access to promotional assets and media kits.

Your Rights

You can ask for your information to be updated or removed at any time.

  • Contact us to correct or delete your details.
  • Opt-out of future newsletters at any time.
  • Use the contact email on the site for privacy requests.
