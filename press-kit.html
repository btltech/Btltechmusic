Artist Bio

BTLTECH Music

BTLTECH Music is home to two artists. BTLTECH is an Afro-fusion artist creating energetic Afrobeats, dancehall, motivational music, and family celebration anthems, connecting Lagos street energy with diaspora life. Choko King is the label's bold, ragga-inspired Afro-dancehall artist, full of playful street-anthem energy. The team also builds Lyrid, a lyric-video app.

Story Angles

  • Lagos-to-London perspective with street grit and diaspora ambition
  • Afro-fusion built from dancehall energy, motivation, and celebration
  • Two distinct artists — BTLTECH and Choko King — under one label
  • Artist-built tech: the Lyrid lyric-video app
