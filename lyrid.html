Preview For Mac · Lyrid

Lyrid

A lyric-video app in development for Mac.

Lyrid turns any song into a share-ready lyric video. Drop in your track, sync the words to the beat, pick a template, and export clean captions and crisp visuals for Reels, TikTok, Shorts, and YouTube. The app is still being finished, so this page is a product preview and early-access point rather than a public download page.

Features

Everything you need to make a lyric video

Built for artists and teams who want clean lyric videos without heavy editing software.

🎵

Beat-synced lyrics

Tap along or fine-tune timing so every line lands exactly on the beat.

🎨

Ready-made templates

Pick from styled fonts, colors, and motion presets that look pro out of the box.

📤

Made for socials

Export vertical, square, or wide for Reels, TikTok, Shorts, and YouTube.

⚡

Native on Mac

A fast, native macOS app — go from audio to finished video in minutes, no clunky timeline.

🔤

Smart captions

Clean, readable text with highlight-on-beat so fans can sing along.

⬇️

High-quality export

Download crisp video files ready to post or share anywhere.
